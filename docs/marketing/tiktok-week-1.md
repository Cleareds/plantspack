# TikTok content calendar - Week 1 (Apr 29 - May 5, 2026)

Goal of week 1: ship 7 videos, learn which format converts, do not optimise for virality yet.

## Account setup (Day 0, before posting)

- Handle: `@plantspack` (mirror across IG Reels and YouTube Shorts).
- Bio: `Vegan-only places, worldwide. No omni-washing. -> plantspack.com`
- Profile pic: logo on solid background (not the wordmark - too thin at small sizes).
- Link in bio: `plantspack.com` (not a Linktree - one click less = higher conversion).
- Business account: yes, so you can see analytics. Category: Travel or Food.

## Posting rules for the week

- Post once per day, between 18:00 and 21:00 local time (peak EU/UK evening scroll).
- Vertical 9:16, under 30 seconds for week 1. Hook in first 1.5 seconds.
- On-screen text always (most viewers watch muted).
- No voiceover required - screen recording + trending sound is enough.
- One CTA only, on the last frame: "Free vegan map - link in bio".
- Cross-post the same export to IG Reels and YouTube Shorts the same evening. Different hashtags per platform; same video file is fine.

## Recurring formats (rotate, do not invent new ones every day)

1. **City top 5** - screen recording of the map zooming into a city, then 5 quick cuts of place cards.
2. **Hidden gem** - one place, 3-5 photos, one sentence on why it's special.
3. **"Did you know" map fact** - "There are 412 fully vegan spots in London on PlantsPack" with a zoom-out reveal.
4. **Behind-the-scenes** - founder POV, 15 seconds of you adding/verifying a place. Builds trust, not viral, but high follow rate.
5. **Travel hack** - "Going to [city] vegan? Save this map" with a quick demo of the trip-save feature.

## Production workflow (~30 min per video once template is set)

1. Pick the place(s) from `/admin` or city page.
2. Screen-record the map and listing on a phone or QuickTime (mobile screen mirror).
3. Pull 3-5 photos from the listing.
4. Edit in CapCut (free): cuts, text, trending sound from TikTok's own library (NOT external music - copyright risk).
5. Export 1080x1920, 30fps.
6. Post on TikTok first, then download the watermark-free version from CapCut and upload to IG Reels + YT Shorts.

## Hashtag strategy

- 4-6 hashtags max. More dilutes reach on TikTok in 2026.
- Always mix: 1 broad (`#vegan`), 1 city (`#veganlondon`), 1 niche (`#hiddenfoodgems`, `#vegantravel`).
- Do NOT use `#fyp`, `#viral`, `#foryoupage` - downranked.

## What to measure at end of week 1

- Views per video (compare day-by-day, not absolute).
- Follow rate = new followers / views. Target: > 0.5% by end of week 2.
- Profile visits (TikTok analytics shows this).
- Bio link clicks (TikTok shows for business accounts).
- Signups attributed: add `?utm_source=tiktok` to the bio link, check signups in Supabase.

## Decision rule for week 2

- If one format clearly out-performs (3x the views), week 2 = 5 of that format + 2 experiments.
- If everything is flat (<200 views average), the issue is hooks, not format. Spend week 2 only on improving the first 1.5 seconds.
- Do not pivot to YouTube long-form, podcasts, or blog content based on week 1 results. One platform until something works.

## Hard rules

- No fake urgency, no "I quit my job" bait, no faces in thumbnails for clickbait.
- Never claim a place is fully vegan in a video unless verified in DB - one wrong claim = trust gone.
- No music with vocals from copyrighted tracks. Use TikTok's commercial library only.
- Do not buy followers, likes, or engagement. Algorithm punishes it within 48h.
